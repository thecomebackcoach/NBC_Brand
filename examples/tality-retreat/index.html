A new role
// the mission manager

Who keeps this alive?

Every mission needs a champion. Not a poster. Not an email. A person. Someone who notices when cups are getting filled and when they're not — and asks the question out loud.

Not HR.
// not a job title or extra duty
Not marketing.
// not a campaign or a poster
A noticer.
// someone who sees the small stuff and names it in the team meeting
In every location.
// at least one. could be the owner, could be your front desk.

Now what
// we keep working it together.

Try it on.

  • Use the five tools — alignment, decision, design, elevating, do-the-right-thing.
  • Ask yourself the question. Did that fill a cup? Honestly.
  • Notice when it feels natural and when it feels forced. Both are data.
  • Bring what you find back — to your team, to your partners, to the next time we meet.
